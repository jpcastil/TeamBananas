General Mental Health Information

There are many reputable hotlines you can contact for answers to questions about mental health or mental illness, including:

Health Hotlines Email Phone
The National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI) info@nami.org (800) 950-6264
Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA) (800) 662-4357
National Institute of Mental Health (NIMH) (866) 615-6464


Other Helplines

While there are many emergency mental health hotlines, if you or someone you care about is having thoughts of suicide, call 911 for immediate help.

Some of the reputable 24-hour mental health hotlines that can provide you with support, education, and resources include:

Health Hotlines Website Phone
Mental Health America Hotline http://www.mentalhealthamerica.net/ Text MHA to 741741
National Suicide Prevention Lifeline https://suicidepreventionlifeline.org/ (800) 273-8255
Crisis Text Line http://www.crisistextline.org/textline/ CONNECT to 741741
The Samaritans http://samaritansnyc.org/24-hour-crisis-hotline/ (212) 673-3000
Veterans Crisis Line https://www.veteranscrisisline.net/ (800) 273-8255
