Local Health Resources: San Luis Obispo

All our locations are handicap accessible and close to public transportation. Services are available in English and Spanish at all locations. Other spoken language or ASL needs can be met as needed. Mental Health clinic treatment teams have openings and are continually accepting new referrals. Clinic staff offer Therapy, Rehabilitation, Case Management, Medication Support and Crisis Intervention services.

Staff at Mental Health Clinics and Specialty Programs have specialized training to assist with a wide variety of phase of life concerns (including early childhood, transitional aged youth, and older adult issues). We use a trauma informed care model and can help with recovery from past abuse, trauma, or discrimination. All staff have received cultural competency training and are sensitive to culture, gender and sexual identity, and lifestyle choices and provide services in an open, caring, compassionate manner. Many staff are bilingual and/or bicultural.
